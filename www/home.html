About the WMS

WMS is a workshop mobile application that can help to solve issues for workshop management and customers. This application will help the customers to manage their vehicle service on time. Customers can know the components that have been replace or repair. Users need to register for an account to upload the car descriptions in the application. The vehicle status will appear through the app. Then, the app will give notice when the vehicle needs to get service. The user will not miss out the date for the service.
